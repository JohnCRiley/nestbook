How it works

Stop paying commission. Start keeping what you earn.

NestBook gives independent B&Bs, gîtes, lodges and holiday rentals their own booking page — so guests book directly with you. Zero commission. Always.

Start free — no card needed →

Free plan available · Pro from £19/month · 30-day trial

Booking.com charges 15–18% commission on every booking. Airbnb now charges 15.5% host-only fee (new 2026 structure). On 20 bookings at £150/night, that's over £500 straight to them — not you. NestBook charges a flat monthly fee. Commission: zero.

Up and running in 20 minutes

Four steps. No technical knowledge needed.

1

Sign up free

Create your account in under a minute. No credit card, no contract, no commitment. The Free plan is free forever — upgrade only if you want more.

Takes about 60 seconds
2

Add your property and rooms

Enter your property details, add your rooms or bedrooms, set your prices, upload photos and write descriptions. NestBook works for B&Bs, gîtes, lodges, villas, glamping and everything in between.

Takes about 15–20 minutes
3

Share your booking page

Every NestBook property gets a free booking page — your rooms, your photos, your availability calendar, your prices. Share the link anywhere.

Your page: nestbook.io/book/your-property-name
4

Add a Book Now button to Facebook

Copy your booking page link and add it as a Book Now button on your Facebook business page. Takes two minutes. Guests click it and book directly with you — no Airbnb, no Booking.com, no commission.

Works on Facebook, Instagram, WhatsApp, email signatures — anywhere
