This Privacy Policy explains how NestBook collects, uses and protects your personal data when you use our service. We are committed to GDPR compliance and to handling your data with care and respect.

1. Who we are (Data Controller)

NestBook is the data controller for the personal data processed through the NestBook service. You can contact us at hello@nestbook.io.

2. Data we collect

Account information

When you register, we collect your name, email address, and hashed password. We also store your property name and property type.

Booking and guest data

As part of providing the service, we store the booking records, room details, and guest information (name, email, phone) that you enter into NestBook. You are the data controller for your guests' personal data; we process it on your behalf as a data processor.

Payment information

We do not store credit card details. Payments are processed by Stripe, who holds payment card data under PCI-DSS compliance. We receive confirmation of successful payment and a subscription status from Stripe.

Technical data

Our server logs may record IP addresses, browser type, and request timestamps for security and debugging purposes. We do not use analytics tools or tracking cookies.

3. How we use your data

We use your personal data only to:

  • Create and manage your NestBook account
  • Provide and improve the NestBook service
  • Send transactional emails (booking confirmations, welcome emails) where enabled
  • Process subscription payments via Stripe
  • Respond to your support requests and contact form submissions
  • Comply with legal obligations

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes. We do not use your data to build advertising profiles.

Our legal basis for processing is: contract performance (to provide the service you signed up for), legitimate interests (security, debugging), and consent (for optional email communications).

4. Where we store your data

All NestBook application data is stored on servers operated by Hetzner Online GmbH, located in data centres in the European Union (Germany and Finland). Hetzner is GDPR-compliant and we have a Data Processing Agreement in place with them.

Your data does not leave the EU/EEA in the normal course of service operation.

5. Third-party services

We use a small number of third-party processors, each subject to a Data Processing Agreement:

  • Stripe (payments) — processes subscription payment card data. Stripe is PCI-DSS Level 1 certified and GDPR-compliant. Privacy policy: stripe.com/privacy.
  • Resend (transactional email) — used to send booking confirmation and welcome emails. Resend processes recipient email addresses and email content. Privacy policy: resend.com/legal/privacy-policy.
  • Zoho (support email) — our support mailbox hello@nestbook.io is hosted by Zoho. Messages you send to us are stored in Zoho Mail. Privacy policy: zoho.com/privacy.html.

We do not use Google Analytics, Facebook Pixel, or any other advertising or analytics trackers.

6. Data retention

We retain your account data for as long as your account is active. If you close your account, we will delete or anonymise your personal data within 90 days, except where we are required to retain it for legal or tax compliance purposes (typically up to 7 years for financial records).

Server logs are retained for a maximum of 30 days.

7. Your rights under GDPR

Under the General Data Protection Regulation, you have the following rights:

Right of access

Request a copy of all personal data we hold about you.

Right to rectification

Ask us to correct inaccurate or incomplete personal data.

Right to erasure

Request deletion of your personal data ("right to be forgotten").

Right to portability

Receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format.

Right to object

Object to processing based on legitimate interests.

Right to restrict

Request that we limit how we process your data in certain circumstances.

To exercise any of these rights, email us at hello@nestbook.io. We will respond within 30 days. We may ask you to verify your identity before fulfilling a request.

8. Cookies

We use only essential cookies and browser localStorage. We do not use tracking, advertising, or analytics cookies. For full details, see our Cookie Policy.

9. Children

NestBook is not directed at children under 18. We do not knowingly collect personal data from anyone under 18. If you believe we have inadvertently collected data from a minor, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

10. Contact & complaints

For any privacy-related questions or to exercise your rights, contact us at hello@nestbook.io.

If you are unhappy with how we have handled your data, you have the right to lodge a complaint with your national data protection authority. In the UK this is the Information Commissioner's Office (ICO) at ico.org.uk. In the EU, contact your local supervisory authority.
