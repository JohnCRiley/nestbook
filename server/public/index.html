Features

Everything you need. Nothing you don't.

Built for owners who want to spend time with their guests, not fighting software.

📅
Visual Calendar
Week-view availability grid — rooms across the top, dates down the side. Click any cell to book or view details instantly.
👥
Guest Records
A full guest directory with contact details, stay history, notes and total spend — searchable by name or email.
🔌
Booking Widget
One script tag turns your existing website into a direct booking engine — no third-party fees, no commission.
📶
Works Offline
Check guests in and manage bookings even without internet. Everything syncs when you reconnect.
🔑
Owner & Staff Roles
Invite reception staff with limited access. Owners keep full control over settings, pricing and reports.
🌍
5 Languages Built In
The dashboard and the guest-facing booking widget both work in English, French, Spanish and German — no plugins needed.
