Features

Everything you need. Nothing you don't.

Built for owners who want to spend time with their guests, not fighting software.

Visual Calendar
Week-view availability grid — rooms across the top, dates down the side. Click any cell to book or view details instantly.
Guest Records
A full guest directory with contact details, stay history, notes and total spend — searchable by name or email.
Booking Widget
One script tag turns your existing website into a direct booking engine — no third-party fees, no commission.
Works Offline
Check guests in and manage bookings even without internet. Everything syncs when you reconnect.
Owner & Staff Roles
Invite reception staff with limited access. Owners keep full control over settings, pricing and reports.
5 Languages Built In
The dashboard and the guest-facing booking widget both work in English, French, Spanish, German and Dutch — no plugins needed.
Financial Reports & Exports
Generate revenue reports, export booking data to CSV or PDF, and download your guest contact list — all filtered by date range and property.
Staff Activity Audit Log
Every login, booking, check-in, cancellation and settings change is logged with a timestamp, staff name and IP address — giving owners a full accountability trail.
Direct Booking Page
Every property gets its own booking page at nestbook.io/book/your-name. Add it to Facebook's 'Book Now' button, share it on Instagram, or send it via WhatsApp — no website needed.
Multi plan
Resort-style room charging
Bar, restaurant, shop and activities charged directly to the room. Full itemised folio at checkout. Dedicated portal for service staff.
