Getting started with NestBook

Everything you need to set up your property, add rooms and start taking bookings — in five simple steps.

Setup guide

Five steps to your first booking

Follow these steps in order. Most people are up and running in under 15 minutes.

1

Add your property details

Go to Settings in the left sidebar. Fill in your property name, property type, address, check-in time, check-out time and preferred currency. Click Save changes when done.

Your currency and language settings affect guest-facing emails and booking confirmations.
2

Add your rooms

Go to Rooms and click + Add Room. Fill in the room name, type (double, twin, suite…), price per night and capacity. Click Save. Repeat for each room in your property.

You can also add amenities (WiFi, en-suite, balcony…) to help guests choose the right room.
3

Add the booking widget to your website

Go to Settings and scroll down to the Embed Widget section. Copy the script tag shown there and paste it into your website's HTML just before the closing </body> tag. Guests can then book directly from your website — with no commission.

The Embed Widget section is available on the Pro plan. Try the live demo first at nestbook.io/widget-test.html.
4

Start taking bookings

Share your website with guests. When a guest completes a booking through your widget, it will appear in your Bookings dashboard automatically. You and your guest will both receive an email confirmation.

You can also add bookings manually from the Bookings page — useful for phone or email reservations.
5

Manage your bookings

Use the Calendar page for a colour-coded visual overview of all rooms and dates. Use Bookings for a full searchable list of all reservations. Use Guests to view customer records and contact details.

The Calendar works offline too — perfect for rural properties with unreliable internet.

FAQ

Frequently asked questions

What if I forget my password?

On the sign-in page, click Forgot password? and enter your email address. You'll receive a reset link within a few minutes. If you don't receive it, check your spam folder or email us at hello@nestbook.io.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Go to Settings in your dashboard, scroll to the Subscription section, and click Cancel subscription. You'll keep full access until the end of your current billing period — no immediate loss of service.

How do I add staff members?

Go to Settings and scroll to the Access & Roles section. Click Invite Staff Member and enter their email address. Staff accounts have access to the Calendar and Bookings but cannot change property settings. This feature is available on the Pro plan.

Is my data secure?

Yes. NestBook is fully GDPR compliant. All data is stored on servers in the European Union (Hetzner, Germany). Connections are encrypted with HTTPS. We never sell your data or your guests' data to third parties. See our Privacy Policy for full details.

How do I manage multiple properties?

The Multi plan lets you manage up to 5 properties from a single account. Go to Settings and scroll to the Properties section to add a new property. Once added, use the property switcher in the sidebar to move between properties — each has its own rooms, bookings, and calendar. All your properties can also be viewed together in the cross-property calendar.
