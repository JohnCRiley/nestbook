Cookie Policy

Last updated: April 2026

🛡️
No advertising. No behavioural tracking.

The NestBook app uses only essential cookies. Our public website uses Google Analytics 4 for anonymised traffic analysis. We do not use advertising cookies, retargeting cookies, or any behavioural tracking.

What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored in your browser when you visit a website. They allow the website to remember information about your visit — such as whether you are logged in — and are essential for many website features to function correctly.

Cookies we use

We use a minimal set of cookies, all of which are strictly necessary for the service to operate:

Cookie / Storage Purpose Duration Type
nb-token Authentication — keeps you logged in to the NestBook dashboard. Stored in localStorage. 7 days Essential
nb-lang Language preference — remembers the language you selected. Stored in localStorage. Persistent Essential / Preference
nb-cookie-consent Records that you have dismissed the cookie notice. Stored in localStorage. Persistent Essential
Stripe cookies Payment processing — set by Stripe when you complete a subscription payment. Used for fraud prevention and payment security only. Session / 1 year Essential
_ga, _ga_* Google Analytics 4 — anonymised website analytics on our public pages. Tracks page views and traffic sources. No personal or booking data is collected. 2 years Analytics

Cookies we do NOT use

  • Advertising or retargeting cookies
  • Social media tracking pixels
  • Third-party profiling or behavioural tracking of any kind

How to manage cookies

You can control or delete cookies through your browser settings. Note that disabling essential cookies will affect the functionality of the NestBook app. To opt out of Google Analytics specifically, you can use the Google Analytics opt-out browser add-on.

  • Chrome: Settings → Privacy and security → Cookies and other site data
  • Firefox: Settings → Privacy & Security → Cookies and Site Data
  • Safari: Preferences → Privacy → Manage Website Data
  • Edge: Settings → Cookies and site permissions

You can also delete the NestBook localStorage entries at any time using your browser's developer tools (Application → Local Storage).

Questions?

If you have any questions about our use of cookies, please contact us at hello@nestbook.io.
