About NestBook

We're a small, passionate team building the simplest, most affordable booking software for small European properties.

Our story

NestBook was born out of frustration. We watched small B&B and gîte owners struggle with booking software built for large hotel chains — overpriced, overcomplicated, and indifferent to the realities of running a 4-room guest house in the French countryside or a coastal B&B in Cornwall.

So we built something different. NestBook is designed from the ground up for owners who want to spend time with their guests, not fighting software. No month-long onboarding. No features you'll never use. Just what you need to run your property beautifully.

Our mission

To give every small hospitality business in Europe access to professional, elegant booking tools — regardless of their technical skill or budget. We believe a two-room gîte in Burgundy deserves the same quality of software as a 200-room city hotel.

What we believe in

Simplicity first

Every feature we add must make your life simpler, not more complicated. If it doesn't, it doesn't ship.

Privacy by design

Your data and your guests' data stays in Europe. We use EU hosting (Hetzner), never sell data, and never use tracking cookies.

Fair pricing

No commissions on bookings. No lock-in. A flat monthly rate that makes sense for small businesses. Free to start, always.

Built for Europe

Five languages, GDPR compliance built-in, and pricing in euros. We're made for European operators, by people who understand European hospitality.

The team

We're a small, independent team. No venture capital, no growth-at-all-costs mindset. Just a focused group of developers and hospitality enthusiasts building something we'd want to use ourselves.

Questions? We'd love to hear from you. Reach us any time at hello@nestbook.io.
